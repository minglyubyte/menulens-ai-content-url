ScanMyMenu Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Welcome to ScanMyMenu. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign in using Apple Sign-In, we receive your Name, Email Address, and a unique User ID.
  • User-Uploaded Content: We collect menu photos that you scan using your device's Camera or Photo Library, meal or dish photos you upload for calorie analysis and tracker features, and messages you send to our AI assistant features.
  • Assistant and Session Data: We may store assistant conversation history, assistant session context, and limited cross-surface assistant state in order to provide menu, tracker, and help assistant features.
  • Location Data: If you grant permission, we access your precise location in order to automatically identify nearby restaurants and tag scanned menus. This data is processed in real-time.
  • Purchase and Entitlement Data: We track your in-app subscription purchases, restore events, and entitlement status to manage free-tier access, including the free tracker history window of the last 14 days, as well as Premium Monthly access and Premium Annual access.
  • Usage and Security Signals: We may collect usage logs and related security signals, such as scan counts, dish visualization requests, assistant request volume, device/session activity, tracker history lock interactions, and similar technical metadata, to enforce fair-use limits, detect abnormal behavior, prevent fraud, and protect service reliability.
  • Dietary Preferences and Goals: Through your "Taste Profile", we collect dietary tags, allergies, food preferences, and selected nutrition goals such as macro targets in order to personalize your AI recommendations and tracking experience.
  • Derived Menu, Dish, and Meal Data: We may store structured menu facts, dish descriptions, ingredient information, nutrition estimates, meal component data, daily summaries, and similar AI-derived outputs generated from uploaded content.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is strictly used for App Functionality. Specifically:

  • To securely authenticate your account across devices.
  • To process uploaded menu and meal images using Artificial Intelligence and extract details.
  • To generate and cache structured menu, dish, and meal data from uploaded content.
  • To estimate nutrition information such as calories and macros from uploaded meal photos and support tracker summaries and progress views.
  • To generate AI dish visualizations and reuse them across the app for consistency and performance.
  • To provide personalized responses via the AI assistant based on your Taste Profile, nutrition goals, and assistant session context.
  • To store assistant context needed to support menu, tracker, and help assistant experiences across app surfaces.
  • To determine the restaurant associated with a scanned menu using location services.
  • To manage free-tier limits, including the current free daily scan allowance, the 14-day free tracker history window, and the recent scan-history cap, as well as subscription access, restore purchases, and Premium entitlements, including the current dish visualization allowance.
  • To monitor abnormal, automated, fraudulent, or abusive activity and protect the reliability and security of the service. This may include automated temporary service adjustments, such as reduced request speeds or performance downgrades, during periods of excessive usage.

3. Third-Party Services and AI Processing

  • Artificial Intelligence Providers: Menu images, meal images, and chat text inputs are sent securely to third-party AI providers (such as Google). They are restricted from using your data to train their models.
  • Location Services: Data is transmitted to the Google Places API to fulfill restaurant matching. This is not stored permanently or traced to your personal identity.
  • Cloud Storage: Uploaded menu images and uploaded meal photos are stored securely using Cloudflare R2 object storage as user-linked data needed for app functionality.
  • AI-Generated Visual Outputs: AI-generated dish visualizations are generated by the platform, are owned by ScanMyMenu, and may be retained, displayed, cached, and reused across the app for performance, consistency, and cost efficiency. These generated outputs are not treated as user-owned personal data solely because a user triggered their creation.
  • User Content License for Processing: By uploading menu or meal photos, you grant ScanMyMenu the rights necessary to process, store, and use that uploaded content to provide app functionality, including deriving structured menu information and generating AI outputs from it.
  • Storage Separation: User-uploaded images are stored as user-linked content, while AI-generated dish images may be stored separately as reusable platform assets.

4. App Store Privacy Declarations

In accordance with Apple's App Store Guidelines, we confirm that the data we collect is NOT used for Tracking purposes.

5. Data Retention and Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account directly through the settings within the ScanMyMenu app. Account deletion removes user-linked data from our active systems, including account and profile data, taste profile data, menu scans and uploaded menu images associated with your account, tracker records and uploaded meal photos associated with your account, and assistant conversations, assistant session records, and assistant global state associated with your account.

We may retain AI-generated dish visualizations owned by ScanMyMenu, de-identified or non-user-linked structured menu and dish data, de-identified or non-user-linked generation logs, and records we are required or permitted to retain for legal obligations, fraud prevention, security, backup handling, or the establishment, exercise, or defense of legal claims. User-linked uploaded menu and meal images are deleted from our active systems on account deletion subject to legal, security, and backup limitations, while platform-owned generated outputs may remain because they are not user-specific records.

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants you the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete your data, and the right to opt-out of the sale of personal data.
